FAQ

Common questions before working with a CPA.

What does CPA-led guidance mean?

A Certified Public Accountant brings licensed professional training, tax knowledge, and accountability to tax, accounting, and advisory work.

Is VisionPoint currently accepting new clients?

The website is still in progress and is not yet an active intake channel. The temporary email is listed on the contact page for early inquiries.

What documents should clients prepare?

Prior-year returns, tax notices, business records, payroll reports, bank statements, 1099s, W-2s, K-1s, and major transaction documents are a good starting point.

Why work with a CPA before tax season?

Planning before deadlines gives more time to review estimates, deductions, entity structure, retirement contributions, and record quality.
