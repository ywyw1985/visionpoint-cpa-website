Tax document checklist

  • Prior-year tax return and notices from tax agencies.
  • W-2, 1099, K-1, brokerage, retirement, and rental activity statements.
  • Business income, expenses, asset purchases, payroll reports, and loan statements.
  • Charitable giving, education costs, mortgage interest, property taxes, and estimated tax payments.

Monthly accounting checklist

Keep bank and credit card accounts connected, upload receipts for unusual purchases, review open invoices, note owner transfers, and flag any new loans, assets, contractors, or payroll changes.
